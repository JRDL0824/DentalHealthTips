Dental Health Tips
Don’t go to bed without
brushing your teeth
Brush properly
Don’t neglect your tongue
Use a fluoride toothpaste
Treat flossing as important
as brushing
Don’t let flossing difficulties
stop you
Consider mouthwash
Drink more water
Eat crunchy fruits and
vegetables
Limit sugary and acidic foods
See your dentist at least
twice a year
